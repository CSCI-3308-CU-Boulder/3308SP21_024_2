PlaySync is a software tool used to migrate/synchronize playlists across multiple music streaming platforms. These platforms may include but are not limited to, Spotify, Apple Music, Pandora, Amazon Music, and YouTube Music. This tool requires users to log in to their respective music platform accounts and specify which platform they wish to migrate from and which platform to migrate to. Users must specify which playlists they wish to migrate. Users may view the contents of each playlist before transferring.
